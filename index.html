About Miss Sherry!

Welcome to my world of Tarot! I'm Miss Sherry, your friendly Tarot card reader hailing from the beautiful state of Tennessee. As you get to know me better, you'll discover that Tarot isn't just a passion of mine; it's a calling, a way of life, and a means through which I connect with the energies of the universe. Born and raised in the heart of Tennessee, I bring the warmth, hospitality, and charm of the South to every reading. It's not just about the cards; it's about creating a comfortable and welcoming space where you can explore life's questions and challenges. Just like a glass of sweet tea on a sunny day, my readings are meant to soothe your soul and refresh your spirit. When I'm not reading Tarot cards, you can find me enjoying a glass of sweet tea on my porch, savoring the tranquility of Tennessee's countryside. There's something magical about sipping sweet tea as the sun sets and the fireflies dance in the warm summer evenings. It's a moment of connection with nature and a reminder of the simple joys in life.

You can now talk to Miss Sherry herself without worry of being redirected to a random unknown advisor. With decades of experience and an understanding of life that only motherhood can offer, let Miss Sherry share her wisdom with you.
